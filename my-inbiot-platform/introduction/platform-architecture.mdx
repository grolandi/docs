4. Settings: You can access the account settings from anywhere on the plaform by clicking on the menu on the right side of the navbar and seleccting “Settings”
   <Frame>
     ![Image](/images/image-40.png)
   </Frame>
   The Settings sections has 3 sub-sections: Account, Visualization and Units:
   <Tabs>
     <Tab title="Account">
       Here you can change your personal information, upload a logo, restore your password or delete your account.
     </Tab>
     <Tab title="Visualization">
       Here you can personalize how you want to see information like the indicator for each MICA model or whether you want to see the groups floded or unfolded on the general dashboard.
     </Tab>
     <Tab title="Units & Language">
       Here you can define the parameter’s units (for those with more than one) and the data language. This will determine the langage and units for any data downloaded.
     </Tab>
   </Tabs>
